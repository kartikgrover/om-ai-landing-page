Rahu-Ketu Transit 2026: Capricorn & Cancer — Effects for All Ascendants

On December 6, 2026, the lunar nodes undergo a major axis shift: Rahu moves into Capricorn (Makara Rashi) and Ketu moves into Cancer (Karka Rashi). Rahu and Ketu always transit in opposite signs, and they change signs approximately every 18 months. This makes each nodal transit a significant event in Vedic astrology, traditionally associated with karmic themes, obsessions, and spiritual lessons that unfold over the following year and a half.

Unlike the visible planets, Rahu and Ketu are shadow planets (Chaya Graha) — mathematical points where the Moon's orbit intersects the ecliptic. Despite having no physical body, they are traditionally regarded as among the most powerful forces in a birth chart.

Rahu in Capricorn: Ambition Amplified

Capricorn is the sign of structure, discipline, and worldly achievement, ruled by Saturn. When Rahu — the planet of insatiable desire and worldly ambition — enters Capricorn, there is traditionally an intensification of themes around career, status, authority, and material success. Rahu amplifies whatever it touches, and in Saturn's practical, goal-oriented sign, this energy is traditionally directed toward building power, climbing hierarchies, and achieving tangible results.

On a collective level, this transit is traditionally associated with shifts in government structures, corporate ambition, and societal attitudes toward authority and discipline.

Ketu in Cancer: Letting Go of Comfort

Ketu represents detachment, spirituality, and past-life karma. In Cancer — the sign of home, mother, emotions, and comfort — Ketu traditionally encourages releasing emotional attachments, moving beyond the need for security, and finding peace through spiritual practice rather than material comfort.

This placement may bring a sense of restlessness around home and family matters. Traditionally, Ketu in Cancer is seen as an opportunity to develop emotional independence and to process deep-seated feelings that may have been avoided.

Navigating This Transit

Rahu-Ketu transits are traditionally understood as karmic in nature. Unlike Jupiter or Venus transits, which often bring tangible external events, the nodes tend to work on deeper psychological and spiritual levels. Here are some traditional guidelines:

  • Pay attention to the houses involved: The house where Rahu transits may become an area of intense desire and new pursuits. The house where Ketu transits may feel like an area of release or disinterest.
  • Avoid obsessive behavior: Rahu can create tunnel vision. Being aware of this tendency can help you maintain balance.
  • Embrace Ketu's lessons: The areas where Ketu transits may feel uncomfortable precisely because they require letting go. Resistance to this process traditionally creates more difficulty.
  • Consider your natal nodes: If this transit activates your natal Rahu-Ketu axis (for example, if you have Rahu in Cancer or Capricorn natally), the effects may be especially pronounced. This is sometimes called a "nodal return."
  • Check the full picture: As with all transits, the effects depend on your complete birth chart, including the condition of Saturn (Capricorn's ruler) and the Moon (Cancer's ruler) in your horoscope.
