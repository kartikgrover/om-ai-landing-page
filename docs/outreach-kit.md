## What Is Om.AI

Om.AI is an AI-powered Vedic astrology app that reads your birth chart (Kundli) like a classical Jyotishi. Unlike generic horoscope apps that give everyone the same sun-sign prediction, Om.AI calculates your complete chart — all 9 planets, 12 houses, Navamsa (D9), and Vimshottari Dasha timeline — then lets you ask questions in plain language and get personalized answers with specific dates and timing.

## What Makes It Different

- **AI trained on classical texts**: Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra (BPHS), Lal Kitab, KP System, Uttara Kalamrita — not generic content templates
- **Precision astronomy**: Planetary positions calculated to arc-second accuracy with Lahiri Ayanamsa correction
- **Full chart depth**: D1 + D9 charts, Shadbala planetary strength, Ashtakavarga, yoga/dosha detection, Vimshottari Dasha
- **Conversational**: Ask follow-up questions — the AI remembers context from previous readings
- **Timing predictions**: Specific dates based on Dasha periods and transit windows, not vague "this month" predictions
- **Beyond horoscopes**: Ashtakoot compatibility matching (free), Bhagavad Gita with 700 verses + audio, sacred mantras, daily Panchang
- **Earn questions through streaks**: Daily reading builds a streak; milestones at 3, 7, 14, 21, 30, 45, and 60 days earn additional AI questions

## Key Features

1. **AI Astrology Chat** — Ask about career, marriage, finances, health. Get answers referencing your specific planetary placements, active Dasha periods, and upcoming transits.

2. **Birth Chart (Kundli)** — Complete D1 Rashi chart + D9 Navamsa with all planetary positions, house lords, aspects, and dignities. Included with the app.

3. **Compatibility Matching** — Free Ashtakoot (36-point) matching with Manglik Dosha, Nadi Dosha, and Navamsa analysis for both partners.

4. **Daily Horoscope** — Moon-sign based, generated daily from real planetary positions — not recycled content.

5. **Bhagavad Gita** — Complete 18 chapters, 700 verses with Sanskrit, English translation, Hindi transliteration, and audio.

6. **Sacred Mantras** — Gayatri Mantra, Hanuman Chalisa with audio and transliteration.

7. **Daily Panchang** — Tithi, Nakshatra, Yoga, Karana, Rahu Kaal, Yamaganda.
