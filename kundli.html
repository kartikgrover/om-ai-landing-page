What Is a Kundli?

A Kundli (also spelled Kundali) is a Vedic birth chart — a precise map of where the nine planets (Navagraha) were positioned at the exact moment and location of your birth. In Jyotish Shastra, this chart is the foundation for understanding your personality, career trajectory, relationships, health patterns, and the timing of major life events. Every Kundli is unique because even a few minutes difference in birth time shifts the Ascendant (Lagna), which changes the entire house structure of the chart.

Traditionally, a Kundli was prepared by a family pandit or Jyotishi who would consult a Panchang (astronomical almanac) and manually compute planetary longitudes using tables. This process took hours and required deep expertise in both astronomical calculation and textual interpretation. Even experienced practitioners could only cross-reference a limited number of planetary combinations (yogas) from memory.

What Your AI Kundli Includes

Every Kundli generated by Om.AI includes six layers of analysis — from the foundational chart to advanced timing predictions.

Rashi Chart (D1)

Your primary birth chart showing the placement of all nine planets — Sun, Moon, Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn, Rahu, and Ketu — across the twelve houses. Each planet's house, zodiac sign, Nakshatra (lunar mansion), Pada (quarter), and dignity (exalted, debilitated, friendly, enemy, or neutral) are computed and displayed. The Rashi chart reveals the broad patterns of your life — your strengths, challenges, and the areas where each planet's energy manifests.

Navamsa Chart (D9)

The Navamsa is the most important divisional chart in Vedic astrology. Derived by dividing each sign into nine equal parts, it reveals the deeper quality of your planetary placements — especially for marriage, relationships, and spiritual growth. Om.AI generates your D9 chart alongside the D1 and highlights Vargottama planets (those occupying the same sign in both charts), which are considered exceptionally strong and auspicious in classical texts.

Vimshottari Dasha Timeline

The Dasha system is what makes Vedic astrology uniquely powerful at timing predictions. Based on the Moon's Nakshatra at birth, the Vimshottari Dasha maps your entire life into planetary periods — each ruled by a specific planet for a fixed duration. Om.AI calculates your complete Mahadasha (major period) and Antardasha (sub-period) timeline, showing which planet's energy dominates each phase of your life and when transitions occur.

Ashtakavarga-Powered Analysis

Ashtakavarga is a classical scoring system from BPHS that quantifies the strength of each house in your chart. Each of the seven planets and the Ascendant contributes Bindus (points) to each house, producing strength scores that reveal where life flows easily and where it presents more friction. Om.AI's AI uses Ashtakavarga calculations behind the scenes when analyzing your chart and answering your questions — giving its interpretations the precision of classical house-strength scoring without requiring you to understand the numbers yourself.

Yoga Detection

Yogas are specific planetary combinations that indicate particular life outcomes. Om.AI's AI scans your chart for hundreds of yogas from classical texts — Raj Yoga (power and authority), Gaja Kesari Yoga (wisdom and reputation), Pancha Mahapurusha Yogas, Dhana Yoga (wealth), and Viparita Raj Yoga (success through adversity), among others. Each detected yoga includes a plain-language explanation of its effects and when it is likely to activate based on your Dasha timeline.
