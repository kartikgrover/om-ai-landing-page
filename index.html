How does the AI astrology work?

Om.AI's astrology engine works in three steps. First, you enter your birth date, exact time of birth, and city of birth. The app then calculates your complete Kundli — including your Lagna (ascendant), positions of all 9 Vedic planets (Navagraha) across the 12 houses, Navamsa (D9) chart, and active Dasha periods. Second, the AI analyzes your chart using principles from authoritative Vedic texts including Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra, Jataka Parijata, and Lal Kitab. It identifies yogas (beneficial combinations), doshas (afflictions like Manglik or Kaal Sarp), and planetary strengths. Third, when you ask a question — for example, 'When will I get married?' or 'Is this a good time to change jobs?' — the AI cross-references your birth chart with current planetary transits to give you a personalized answer. Every response is specific to your unique chart, not a generic sun-sign horoscope.

What does the Compatibility feature do?

The Compatibility feature performs a comprehensive Vedic marriage compatibility analysis between two people. It uses the traditional Ashtakoot (8-point) matching system, which scores compatibility across 8 dimensions: Varna (spiritual compatibility), Vashya (mutual attraction), Tara (health and well-being), Yoni (physical compatibility), Graha Maitri (mental compatibility), Gana (temperament), Bhakoot (love and finances), and Nadi (genetic compatibility). The maximum score is 36 points, with 18+ generally considered favorable for marriage. Beyond Ashtakoot, the app also checks for specific doshas like Manglik Dosha and Nadi Dosha that could affect the relationship, and provides traditional Vedic remedies where applicable. Simply enter both partners' birth details and receive a detailed report with scores, explanations, and personalized insights.
