What is Vedic Astrology?

Vedic astrology (Jyotish) is a 5,000-year-old system originating from ancient Indian scriptures. Unlike Western astrology, it uses the sidereal zodiac — the actual position of stars — making it astronomically precise.

It analyzes your birth chart across 12 houses covering every aspect of life: career, relationships, health, wealth, and spiritual growth. Classical texts like Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra, Lal Kitab, and Uttara Kalamrita form its foundation.

How Our AI Works

Om.AI is trained on the core Vedic astrology texts — the same scriptures that traditional astrologers study for decades. Our AI reads your exact birth chart data: planetary positions, house strengths, Mahadasha periods, and transits.

It then translates complex astrological data into clear, actionable guidance — no jargon, no vague predictions. Every response is personalized to your chart, giving you specific timing, remedies, and mantras rooted in authentic Vedic knowledge.
