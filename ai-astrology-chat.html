How the AI Reads Your Chart

Om.AI doesn't generate vague predictions from templates. Here's what happens when you ask a question.

1

Calculates Your Kundli

Using your birth date, time, and location, the app computes precise planetary positions for all 9 Vedic planets (Navagraha) across the 12 houses, including your Rashi chart (D1) and Navamsa chart (D9). Positions are calculated with arc-second precision using the Lahiri Ayanamsa.

2

Analyzes Your Dashas & Transits

The AI identifies your current Vimshottari Mahadasha and Antardasha periods, then overlays today's planetary transits on your birth chart. For timing questions, it scans upcoming transits to find significant dates relevant to your question.

3

Applies Classical Jyotish Rules

Your chart data is interpreted through rules from Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra, Lal Kitab, KP System, and Uttara Kalamrita. The AI checks for yogas, doshas, planetary strengths (Shadbala), house-lord relationships, and aspects — the same analysis a seasoned Jyotishi would perform.

4

Generates a Personalized Answer

The AI synthesizes all of this into a clear, specific answer to your question — with dates, planetary references, and practical guidance. It remembers your previous conversations, so follow-up questions build on earlier readings.
