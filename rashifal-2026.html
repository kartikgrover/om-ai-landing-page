Key Planetary Transits in 2026

2026 is shaped by several major planetary movements that will influence all Moon signs:

Jupiter in Gemini → Cancer (June 2, 2026) — Jupiter continues its transit through Gemini for the first half of 2026, expanding communication, learning, and short travels. Jupiter enters Cancer — its exaltation sign — on June 2, 2026, bringing a powerful shift toward domestic matters, emotional growth, and family blessings. Jupiter then moves into Leo on October 31, 2026, shifting focus toward creativity, leadership, and self-expression.
Saturn in Pisces (through 2026) — Saturn remains in Pisces throughout 2026, continuing to test matters of spirituality, foreign connections, and letting go of outdated patterns. This is Saturn's exalted sign-neighbor, bringing discipline to intuitive and creative pursuits.
Rahu in Aquarius / Ketu in Leo (until December 5, 2026) — Rahu transits Aquarius for most of 2026, amplifying networking, technology, and unconventional paths. Ketu in Leo pushes detachment from ego and recognition-seeking. On December 5, 2026, the axis shifts — Rahu enters Capricorn and Ketu enters Cancer — initiating a new karmic cycle focused on career structures and emotional foundations.
Mercury Retrogrades — Mercury goes retrograde three times in 2026: in February (Aquarius), June (Cancer to Gemini), and in October (Libra). Communication and travel need extra care during these windows.

♈ Aries (Mesha) Rashifal 2026

For Aries Moon sign natives, 2026 brings a year of expanding horizons in the first half and a focus on home and emotional foundations in the second half. Jupiter transiting your 3rd house (Gemini) until mid-year boosts courage, communication skills, and sibling relationships. After June 2, Jupiter moves into Cancer (your 4th house), bringing blessings to domestic life, property matters, and emotional well-being.

Career & Finance

Saturn in your 12th house (Pisces) throughout 2026 asks you to work behind the scenes and build patience. Foreign assignments, research, and remote work may be favored. Expenses could rise, so budgeting is important. After June 2, Jupiter in the 4th house may bring property gains or a change in workspace.

Love & Relationships

The first half favors new connections through communication and travel. Venus transits will periodically activate your 7th house, bringing romantic opportunities. Existing relationships benefit from open, honest dialogue — especially during Mercury retrograde periods when misunderstandings are more likely.

Health & Wellness

Saturn in the 12th house may cause fatigue or sleep-related issues. Regular rest, meditation, and reducing overcommitment are advised. Jupiter's move into Cancer after June may improve emotional resilience and overall vitality.

♉ Taurus (Vrishabha) Rashifal 2026

Taurus Moon sign natives experience Jupiter's blessings in the 2nd house (wealth and family) until mid-2026, followed by its move into the 3rd house (courage and communication). This is a year of accumulating resources first, then applying them through bold action.

Career & Finance

Jupiter in the 2nd house until June supports income growth, savings, and family business. Speech and presentation skills are enhanced. Saturn in the 11th house (Pisces) supports long-term gains through networking and older associates. After Jupiter moves to the 3rd house, short business trips and writing/media opportunities open up.

Love & Relationships

Family harmony is strong in the first half. The 2nd house Jupiter favors domestic happiness, good food, and quality family time. After June 2, siblings and close friends play a bigger role. Romantic prospects may come through social circles or community events.

Health & Wellness

Generally a healthy year. Watch for overindulgence (especially the first half) when Jupiter expands the 2nd house of food and pleasures. Saturn's 11th house transit provides stamina for long-term goals. Regular exercise helps channel Jupiter's expansive energy constructively.

♊ Gemini (Mithuna) Rashifal 2026

2026 is a landmark year for Gemini Moon sign natives. Jupiter transits your own sign until mid-year — a once-in-12-year event that expands personality, confidence, and personal luck. After June 2, Jupiter moves to Cancer (2nd house), shifting focus to wealth accumulation and family.

Career & Finance

The first half is excellent for launching new ventures, rebranding, or taking on leadership roles. Jupiter in Lagna (1st house) blesses efforts with optimism and opportunities. Saturn in the 10th house (Pisces) demands discipline in career but rewards hard work with recognition. After June 2, focus shifts to financial planning and building assets.

Love & Relationships

Jupiter in your sign makes you more attractive and approachable. New relationships started in the first half can have lasting potential. Mercury retrogrades (your ruling planet) in February and June require extra care in communication with partners. Be clear and patient.

Health & Wellness

Jupiter in the 1st house generally improves health and vitality, but may cause weight gain. Saturn in the 10th house can create work stress. Balance ambition with adequate rest. The June Mercury retrograde (in your sign) may bring minor health concerns — don't ignore early symptoms.

♋ Cancer (Karka) Rashifal 2026

Cancer Moon sign natives enter 2026 with Jupiter in the 12th house (expenses and spiritual growth), which shifts to Jupiter in your own sign after June — a powerful and auspicious transit. The second half of 2026 is significantly more favorable than the first.

Career & Finance

The first half may bring overseas opportunities, spiritual retreats, or behind-the-scenes work. Expenses could exceed expectations, so save carefully early. After Jupiter enters Cancer (Lagna), confidence surges, new opportunities multiply, and personal luck improves dramatically. Career advancements are most likely between July and December.

Love & Relationships

Saturn in the 9th house all year brings seriousness to beliefs and philosophy, which may affect relationship dynamics. After June 2, Jupiter in your sign makes you more magnetic and emotionally open. This is a good period for deepening commitments or finding meaningful new connections.

Health & Wellness

First half demands more rest and attention to mental health — the 12th house Jupiter can cause feelings of isolation or confusion. After June, health and energy improve significantly. The Moon being your ruling planet means emotional well-being directly impacts physical health throughout the year.

♌ Leo (Simha) Rashifal 2026

Leo Moon sign natives benefit from Jupiter's 11th house transit (gains, social circles) until mid-year, then experience Jupiter in the 12th house (expenses, foreign, spiritual) after June. The first half is more materially rewarding; the second half is for inner growth.

Career & Finance

Income from multiple sources is possible in the first half — networking, elder siblings, and organizational gains come through Jupiter in the 11th. Saturn in the 8th house demands transformation in how you handle shared resources, inheritance, or debt. After June, foreign opportunities or behind-the-scenes projects may call.

Love & Relationships

Social life thrives in the first half. Friends and community play a larger role in your life. Romantic connections through social events are favored. After June 2, some withdrawal or need for solitude may arise — honor this need while keeping communication open with your partner.

Health & Wellness

Saturn in the 8th house all year requires careful attention to chronic health matters. Don't delay check-ups. The first half's social energy helps mental health. In the second half, prioritize rest, meditation, and spiritual practices. Yoga and pranayama are especially beneficial.

♍ Virgo (Kanya) Rashifal 2026

Virgo Moon sign natives experience Jupiter in the 10th house (career peak) until mid-year, then in the 11th house (gains and fulfillment) after June. This is a professionally rewarding year. Ketu transiting your 12th house (Leo) encourages spiritual growth, foreign connections, and letting go of ego-driven ambitions.

Career & Finance

The first half is career-defining — Jupiter in the 10th house brings recognition, promotion opportunities, and public visibility. Saturn in the 7th house demands patience in partnerships and business dealings. After June 2, Jupiter in the 11th house brings financial rewards for hard work, networking opportunities, and achievement of long-held goals.

Love & Relationships

Saturn in the 7th house all year requires maturity and patience in committed relationships. Partnerships may feel tested but ultimately strengthened. Ketu in your sign encourages letting go of controlling tendencies. After June 2, social expansion through Jupiter in the 11th brings new connections.

Health & Wellness

Ketu in the 12th house (Leo) may bring sleep issues, vague health concerns, or a sense of spiritual seeking. Trust your natural analytical nature to maintain good routines but avoid over-worrying. Saturn's 7th house transit may bring joint or lower back issues. Regular stretching, walks, and staying hydrated help.

♎ Libra (Tula) Rashifal 2026

Libra Moon sign natives enjoy Jupiter in the 9th house (luck, travel, higher learning) until mid-year, shifting to the 10th house (career peak) after June. This is a year of expanding wisdom first, then applying it for career success.

Career & Finance

Jupiter's 9th house transit brings opportunities through mentors, teachers, higher education, and long-distance travel. Saturn in the 6th house helps overcome competitors and debts through disciplined effort. After June 2, Jupiter in the 10th house delivers one of the best career periods in 12 years — pursue promotions, launches, and public roles.

Love & Relationships

The 9th house Jupiter favors relationships with people from different backgrounds or cultures. Travel may spark romantic connections. Father figures or mentors play a positive role. After June 2, career focus may require balancing professional and personal time.

Health & Wellness

Saturn in the 6th house actually strengthens the immune system and ability to fight illness through discipline. This is a good year for starting health routines, fitness programs, or dietary changes. Venus (your ruling planet) transits will periodically boost vitality and appearance.

♏ Scorpio (Vrishchika) Rashifal 2026

Scorpio Moon sign natives experience Jupiter in the 8th house (transformation, research) until mid-year, then in the 9th house (expansion, higher learning) after June. A year of deep internal shifts followed by outward growth and wisdom.

Career & Finance

The first half favors research, investigation, insurance, inheritance, and transformative projects. Financial gains through hidden or unconventional means are possible. Saturn in the 5th house demands disciplined creativity and careful investment. After June 2, Jupiter in the 9th opens doors to higher education, long-distance travel, and spiritual growth.

Love & Relationships

The 8th house Jupiter deepens emotional bonds but may also surface hidden issues that need resolution. Honesty is essential. Saturn in the 5th house can delay romantic developments but creates lasting connections when they do form. Children-related matters may require patience.

Health & Wellness

The 8th house transit requires caution — avoid risky activities, especially in the first half. Mental health awareness is important; Scorpio's tendency toward intensity can amplify during this period. After June, the 9th house Jupiter brings optimism and physical vitality. Outdoor activities and travel support well-being.

♐ Sagittarius (Dhanu) Rashifal 2026

Sagittarius Moon sign natives experience Jupiter (your ruling planet) in the 7th house (partnerships) until mid-year, then in the 8th house (transformation) after June. Relationships take center stage in 2026, followed by a period of deep internal change.

Career & Finance

Jupiter in the 7th house favors business partnerships, client relationships, and collaborative ventures. Saturn in the 4th house demands building strong foundations — real estate, home office, or family business matters need attention. After June, joint finances and investments come into focus through the 8th house transit.

Love & Relationships

The first half is one of the best periods in 12 years for committed partnerships. Marriage, engagement, or deepening an existing relationship is strongly favored. Jupiter in the 7th house as your own ruling planet makes this transit especially powerful. Proposals and commitments made before June carry long-term blessings.

Health & Wellness

Saturn in the 4th house may cause chest, heart, or emotional health concerns. Domestic stress should be managed proactively. The first half's positive Jupiter energy supports good health through partnership activities (couples yoga, walking together). After June, focus on preventive health measures.

♑ Capricorn (Makara) Rashifal 2026

Capricorn Moon sign natives experience Jupiter in the 6th house (overcoming obstacles) until mid-year, then in the 7th house (partnerships, marriage) after June. A year of clearing challenges first, then attracting partnerships and opportunities.

Career & Finance

Jupiter in the 6th house helps defeat competition, resolve legal matters, and overcome workplace challenges. Saturn in your 3rd house supports methodical effort, short business travel, and communication projects. After June 2, Jupiter in the 7th house brings business partnerships, client growth, and collaborative success.

Love & Relationships

The first half focuses on resolving relationship issues and clearing misunderstandings. Healing old wounds is favored. After June 2, Jupiter's move to the 7th house makes the second half excellent for marriage, proposals, or formalizing partnerships. This is a significant relationship period for Capricorn natives.

Health & Wellness

Jupiter in the 6th house is actually favorable for health — it helps overcome illness and supports recovery. Saturn in the 3rd house gives mental toughness. This is a good year to establish health routines. Short walks, breathing exercises, and consistent sleep schedules are especially beneficial.

♒ Aquarius (Kumbha) Rashifal 2026

Aquarius Moon sign natives enjoy Jupiter in the 5th house (creativity, romance, children) until mid-year, then in the 6th house (service, health) after June. The first half is joyful and creative; the second half demands practical discipline.

Career & Finance

Jupiter in the 5th house favors creative industries, entertainment, education, speculation (with care), and projects involving children or youth. Saturn in the 2nd house requires careful financial management — save and invest wisely. After June 2, Jupiter in the 6th supports service-oriented work and overcoming workplace obstacles.

Love & Relationships

The first half is one of the most romantic periods in 12 years for Aquarius natives. Jupiter in the 5th house brings joy, fun, and new romantic experiences. Existing couples may plan for children. Creative expression strengthens bonds. After June 2, the focus shifts to practical relationship responsibilities.

Health & Wellness

The first half sees good health and high spirits. Saturn in the 2nd house may affect diet or speech — be mindful of what you consume. After June 2, Jupiter in the 6th house supports health improvements through structured routines. This is a good period to address any lingering health issues.

♓ Pisces (Meena) Rashifal 2026

Pisces Moon sign natives experience Jupiter in the 4th house (home, comfort, mother) until mid-year, then in the 5th house (creativity, children, romance) after June. Both are positive houses for Jupiter, making 2026 a generally favorable year. Saturn in your own sign continues its once-in-30-years transit.

Career & Finance

The first half favors real estate, home-based work, vehicles, and domestic expansion. Jupiter in the 4th house can bring a new home or significant renovation. Saturn in Pisces demands maturity and long-term planning in everything. After June 2, Jupiter in the 5th house opens creative professional paths and speculative gains.

Love & Relationships

Family bonds strengthen in the first half — mother's support is especially highlighted. Saturn in your sign tests relationships but strengthens authentic ones. After June 2, Jupiter in the 5th house brings romance, creative joy, and possibly news related to children. This is a wonderful period for deepening emotional connections.

Health & Wellness

Saturn in Pisces can cause fatigue, joint issues, and feelings of heaviness. Don't overextend yourself. Jupiter's 4th house transit in the first half supports emotional comfort and mental peace. After June, the 5th house transit improves vitality and joy. Creative hobbies, music, and art serve as excellent therapy.

About Vedic Rashifal

Rashifal (literally "result of the Rashi") refers to horoscope predictions based on your Moon sign in Vedic astrology. Unlike Western astrology which primarily uses the Sun sign, Vedic astrology (Jyotish) considers the Moon sign as the most important factor for daily and yearly predictions. The Moon represents the mind in Vedic tradition, and its sign at birth determines how you experience the effects of planetary transits.
