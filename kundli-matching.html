How Ashtakoot Matching Works

The Ashtakoot system evaluates 8 dimensions of compatibility between two birth charts, scoring a total of 36 points. A score of 18+ is considered favorable for marriage.

1. Varna

1 Point

Spiritual and ego compatibility. Assesses the natural temperament and work ethic alignment between partners.

2. Vashya

2 Points

Mutual attraction and influence. Measures the natural dominance and control dynamics in the relationship.

3. Tara

3 Points

Birth star compatibility. Evaluates health, well-being, and destiny alignment based on Nakshatra positions.

4. Yoni

4 Points

Physical and intimate compatibility. Based on the animal symbol of each partner's birth Nakshatra.

5. Graha Maitri

5 Points

Mental compatibility. Compares the Moon sign lords to assess intellectual and emotional wavelength alignment.

6. Gana

6 Points

Temperament match. Classifies each person as Deva (divine), Manushya (human), or Rakshasa (demon) based on Nakshatra.

7. Bhakoot

7 Points

Emotional bonding and financial prosperity. Evaluates the Moon sign relationship between partners for long-term harmony.

8. Nadi

8 Points

Health and genetic compatibility. The most critical koot — same Nadi for both partners (Nadi Dosha) is a major concern.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ashtakoot Kundli matching?

Ashtakoot Kundli matching (also called Gun Milan) is the traditional Vedic method of assessing marriage compatibility. It evaluates 8 aspects (koots) of compatibility between two birth charts: Varna, Vashya, Tara, Yoni, Graha Maitri, Gana, Bhakoot, and Nadi. Each koot carries a specific number of points, totaling 36. A score of 18 or above is generally considered favorable for marriage.

How many points are needed for a good Kundli match?

In Ashtakoot matching, a score of 18 out of 36 is considered the minimum for a compatible match. Scores between 18-24 are average, 25-32 are very good, and 33-36 are excellent. However, Om.AI goes beyond just the score — it also checks for critical doshas like Nadi Dosha and Bhakoot Dosha, which can override a high score if present.

What is Manglik Dosha and does Om.AI check for it?

Manglik Dosha (Mangal Dosha) occurs when Mars is placed in the 1st, 2nd, 4th, 7th, 8th, or 12th house of the birth chart. It is considered important for marriage compatibility in Vedic astrology. Om.AI checks Manglik Dosha for both partners and analyzes whether any cancellation conditions apply, such as Mars being in its own sign or aspected by benefic planets.

What does Om.AI check beyond the 36-point Ashtakoot score?

Beyond the standard 36-point Ashtakoot score, Om.AI analyzes Manglik Dosha (with cancellation conditions), Nadi Dosha severity, Navamsa (D9) chart compatibility for deeper marital harmony, Dasha period alignment to assess timing compatibility, and 7th house lord strength in both charts. This multi-layered analysis gives a more complete picture than basic score-based matching.

Can I match Kundlis if I don't know exact birth time?

Accurate birth time is important for precise Kundli matching because it determines the Ascendant (Lagna), house placements, and Navamsa chart. If you have an approximate birth time, Om.AI will still generate a report, but the accuracy of house-based analysis and Navamsa compatibility may be affected. For the most reliable results, use the exact birth time from your birth certificate or hospital records.

Is Kundli matching only for arranged marriages?

No. Kundli matching is useful for any couple considering marriage, regardless of how they met. It assesses fundamental compatibility across temperament, emotional bonding, physical compatibility, mutual respect, and long-term harmony. Many couples in love marriages use Kundli matching to understand their relationship dynamics and identify areas that may need attention.

What is Nadi Dosha and why is it important?

Nadi Dosha occurs when both partners have the same Nadi (Aadi, Madhya, or Antya). It carries the highest weight in Ashtakoot matching (8 points) and is considered a serious compatibility concern in traditional Vedic astrology. Om.AI checks for Nadi Dosha and also evaluates whether any classical cancellation conditions apply, providing a nuanced assessment rather than a simple pass/fail.
