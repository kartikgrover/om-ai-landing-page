What is Vedic Horoscope (Rashifal)?

A Vedic horoscope, called Rashifal in Hindi, provides daily predictions based on your Moon sign (Rashi) in Vedic astrology. Unlike Western astrology which uses the Sun sign, Vedic astrology considers your Moon sign as the primary indicator of daily experiences because the Moon changes signs every 2.5 days, making it more sensitive to daily fluctuations. Your Moon sign is determined by which zodiac constellation the Moon was in at the exact time and place of your birth.

The 12 Moon Signs (Rashis)

The 12 Vedic Moon signs are: Mesha (Aries), Vrishabha (Taurus), Mithuna (Gemini), Karka (Cancer), Simha (Leo), Kanya (Virgo), Tula (Libra), Vrishchika (Scorpio), Dhanu (Sagittarius), Makara (Capricorn), Kumbha (Aquarius), and Meena (Pisces). Each sign is ruled by a specific planet — for example, Mesha is ruled by Mars, Vrishabha by Venus, and Karka by the Moon itself.
