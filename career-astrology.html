How Vedic Astrology Reads Your Career

In Jyotish, career is not determined by a single factor. Multiple chart elements combine to reveal your professional path, strengths, and timing.

The 10th House (Karma Bhava)

The 10th house is the primary house of career, profession, and public life. The sign on your 10th house cusp and any planets placed there shape what kind of work suits you, how the world sees your professional identity, and the nature of your ambitions.

The 10th Lord

The planet ruling the sign on your 10th house cusp is your 10th lord. Its placement in a specific house and sign reveals the sector or environment where your career thrives. For example, 10th lord in the 9th house often indicates careers in teaching, law, or foreign lands.

Dashamsa Chart (D10)

The Dashamsa is a divisional chart dedicated to career analysis. It divides each sign into 10 parts (3 degrees each) for a granular view of professional life. Planets strong in D10 indicate areas of career success. The D10 ascendant and its lord point to your career direction and professional reputation.

Planets in the 10th House

Each planet in the 10th house brings distinct career tendencies. Sun favors government or leadership roles, Moon indicates public-facing work, Mars points to engineering or military, Mercury to communication and business, Jupiter to teaching or advisory roles, Venus to arts and luxury industries, and Saturn to disciplined, structured professions.

Mahadasha & Antardasha Influence

Your Vimshottari Dasha timeline determines when career events unfold. The Mahadasha and Antardasha of planets connected to the 10th house trigger job changes, promotions, and professional breakthroughs. Transits of Jupiter and Saturn over the 10th house further refine timing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can astrology predict my career path?

Vedic astrology analyzes your 10th house (house of career), its lord, planets placed in or aspecting it, and your Dashamsa (D10) chart to identify career tendencies and timing. Om.AI's AI reads all these factors from your birth chart and provides personalized career insights based on classical Jyotish principles.

What is the 10th house in astrology and how does it affect career?

The 10th house in Vedic astrology is the house of karma (action), profession, and public reputation. The sign on the 10th house cusp, the planet ruling it, any planets placed in the 10th, and aspects to the 10th house together shape your professional inclinations, work style, and career trajectory. A strong 10th house often indicates career success and recognition.

When is the best time for a job change according to astrology?

Favorable job changes typically occur during Mahadasha or Antardasha periods of planets connected to your 10th house, 6th house (service), or 7th house (business partnerships). Jupiter transits over the 10th house or its lord also support career moves. Om.AI analyzes your specific Dasha periods and upcoming transits to identify the best timing windows.

What is a Dashamsa (D10) chart?

The Dashamsa or D10 chart is a divisional chart in Vedic astrology specifically used for career analysis. It divides each sign into 10 equal parts (3 degrees each) to provide a more detailed picture of your professional life than the birth chart alone. Planets strong in the D10 chart indicate areas of professional success, while the D10 ascendant and its lord reveal your career direction.

Can Om.AI tell me if I should do business or a job?

Your Dasha periods also influence whether a particular phase of life favors independent work or employment.
