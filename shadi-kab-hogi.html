When Will I Get Married? — The Vedic Answer

"Shadi kab hogi" — when will I get married — is one of the oldest questions in Vedic astrology, and it has a real answer. Marriage in your chart is timed by the interaction of five things: the 7th house and its lord, Venus, Jupiter, your active Vimshottari Dasha period, and current planetary transits. When all five align, marriage happens.

The Short Answer

AI marriage prediction can narrow your marriage window to a specific 6-12 month period — not an exact date. The window is found at the intersection of your active Mahadasha/Antardasha and the transit of Jupiter or Saturn over your 7th house or its lord. Most people have 2-3 such windows between their early 20s and early 30s.

Om.AI's AI doesn't read horoscopes by Sun sign. It reads your actual birth chart — the same way a classical Jyotishi would — but in 30 seconds, in plain language, in English or Hindi.

The 5 Things Vedic AI Looks At

Every accurate marriage prediction comes from these five signals. The AI weighs all of them together.

1. The 7th House

The house of marriage. Its lord, occupants, and aspects reveal whether marriage comes early, on time, or late — and what kind of partner is indicated.

2. Venus (Shukra)

The natural significator of love and marriage. A strong Venus brings on-time marriage; a weak or combust Venus delays it. Venus's Dasha is a major trigger.

3. Jupiter (Guru)

For women, Jupiter signifies the husband. Jupiter's transit over your 7th house is a classical marriage trigger — happens once every 12 years.

4. Dasha Period

Your Vimshottari Dasha decides when chart promises activate. The Dasha or Antardasha of your 7th lord, Venus, or Jupiter is when marriage timing peaks.

5. Navamsa (D9)

The divisional chart of marriage. The Navamsa Lagna lord, Venus in D9, and the D9 7th house show whether marriage promises in your D1 chart actually deliver.

+ Transits (Gochar)

Current planetary movements relative to your natal chart. Jupiter and Saturn transits to the 7th house are the biggest real-time triggers.

Late Marriage, Manglik Dosha & Common Delay Signals

If you've been told your shaadi will be late or that you have Manglik Dosha, here's what the AI checks before confirming or ruling it out.

Manglik Dosha

Mars in the 1st, 4th, 7th, 8th, or 12th house from your Lagna creates Manglik Dosha. The AI also checks for cancellation rules — for example, Manglik Dosha is often nullified if both partners are Manglik, or if Jupiter aspects Mars. Many people are wrongly labelled Manglik when the dosha is cancelled.

Saturn on the 7th House

Saturn's aspect on or transit through the 7th house typically slows down marriage — but it also stabilizes it once it happens. Saturn-delayed marriages often happen in the late 20s or early 30s, with a more mature partner.

Weak or Combust Venus

Venus combust (within 10° of the Sun) or debilitated in Virgo can delay love and marriage. The AI looks at whether Venus is rescued by Neechabhanga (debilitation cancellation) or strong Navamsa placement.

Rahu/Ketu in 7th

Rahu in the 7th can bring an unconventional or inter-cultural marriage; Ketu in the 7th can bring detachment or a karmic partner. Both are timing-sensitive — the marriage often comes at a specific Dasha activation.

What is Manglik Dosha and does it delay marriage?

Manglik Dosha occurs when Mars is placed in the 1st, 4th, 7th, 8th, or 12th house from the Lagna. It is traditionally believed to cause delays or friction in marriage. Om.AI's AI identifies Manglik Dosha in your chart and explains its strength, whether it is cancelled by other planetary positions, and how it interacts with your Dasha to time the marriage.

What if my chart shows late or delayed marriage?

Saturn's influence on the 7th house, a debilitated or combust Venus, or strong Manglik Dosha can indicate delay. Om.AI flags these factors and identifies when the delay ends — typically when a favorable Dasha activates, when Jupiter transits your 7th house, or when Saturn's afflicting transit moves on. Many delayed-marriage charts have a clear opening window in the late 20s or early 30s.

Do I need an exact birth time?

Yes — your birth time decides your Lagna (Ascendant) and house cusps, which is the foundation of any marriage prediction. A difference of 4 minutes can shift the Lagna degree noticeably; a difference of 2 hours can change your Ascendant entirely. If you do not know your exact birth time, you can enter an approximate time and ask the AI to identify which Lagna best matches your major life events — but a verified time always gives the most reliable reading.
