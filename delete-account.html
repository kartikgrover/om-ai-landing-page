Delete Your Om.AI Account

Request the deletion of your account and associated data.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Om.AI app and navigate to your profile.
  2. Click on the "Delete Account" button located at the bottom of the profile screen.
  3. Confirm your action by entering your password and clicking "Confirm Delete".
  4. Your account and all associated data will be deleted immediately.

For any further assistance, please contact us.

Data Retention Policy

When you request to delete your account:

  • All personal information including your email, name, and usage data will be deleted from our system.
  • Certain non-identifiable data such as app performance logs may be retained for up to 30 days for technical purposes.

For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
